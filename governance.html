Content Approval Workflow

Establish a clear process for content creation and publishing

Standard Approval Process

1

Content Creation

Team member drafts post with copy, images, and hashtags. Saves as draft in content management system.

2

Manager Review

Direct manager reviews for accuracy, brand voice, and alignment with strategy. Provides feedback or approves.

3

Legal/Compliance Check (if needed)

For regulated industries or sensitive topics, legal team reviews for compliance. Required for financial claims, medical advice, etc.

4

Page Owner Final Approval

Page owner gives final sign-off and schedules post for optimal timing.

5

Publish & Monitor

Post goes live. Team monitors comments and engagement in first 24-48 hours for rapid response.

üí° Streamlined Process for Time-Sensitive Content: Create an expedited approval track for timely posts (news reactions, event coverage). Pre-approve themes and designate who can use fast-track approval.

Quarterly Governance Audit

Review your page governance every quarter to maintain quality and relevance

Audit Checklist

‚ñ° Page Inventory

List all active Company, Showcase, Product, and Service Pages. Verify each still serves a strategic purpose.

‚ñ° Posting Frequency Review

Check if each page meets minimum posting requirements. Flag pages with infrequent updates.

‚ñ° Engagement Analysis

Review engagement rates (likes, comments, shares). Identify high and low performers.

‚ñ° Follower Growth

Track follower growth trends. Investigate pages with declining followers.

‚ñ° Admin Access Review

Verify all admins are current employees with appropriate permissions. Remove departed staff.

‚ñ° Page Information Accuracy

Update company descriptions, logos, locations, and contact information as needed.

‚ñ° Content Strategy Alignment

Ensure content aligns with current business priorities and marketing campaigns.

‚ñ° Duplicate Page Check

Identify any redundant pages that could be consolidated or archived.

‚ñ° Dormant Page Decision

For pages inactive 90+ days: reactivate with content plan, merge with Company Page, or sunset gracefully.

‚ñ° Process Documentation

Document audit findings and action items. Update governance documentation as needed.
