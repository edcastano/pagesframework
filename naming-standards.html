Showcase Page

Format: [Company Name] [Sub-Brand/Division]

Product Page

Use specific product name. Include parent company if product name alone isn't distinctive.

Format: [Product Name] or [Company] [Product Name]

Service Page

Combine company name with service area or practice name.

Format: [Company Name] [Service Area]

Examples: Deloitte Digital, Accenture Strategy, KPMG Cybersecurity

Universal Naming Best Practices

‚úì

Keep It Clear & Concise

Aim for 2-4 words maximum. Long names get truncated in search results and mobile views.

‚úì

Use Proper Capitalization

Follow standard title case or your brand's official capitalization. Avoid ALL CAPS or aLtErNaTiNg CaSe.

‚úì

Be Searchable

Include keywords that your target audience would search for. Think about how people would look for you.

‚úì

Maintain Consistency

Use the same naming pattern across all your pages for brand recognition.

‚úì

Avoid Redundancy

Don't include "Official," "Inc.," "LLC," or "Page" unless legally required or part of your brand.

‚úì

No Special Characters

Avoid symbols like ‚Ñ¢, ¬Æ, ¬©, or excessive punctuation. Stick to letters, numbers, hyphens, and ampersands.

Quick Naming Tips

üí° Before Finalizing Your Name:

  • Search LinkedIn to see if similar names exist
  • Say it out loud - does it sound natural?
  • Check how it looks on mobile (truncates after ~25 characters)
  • Get feedback from colleagues in your target audience
  • Ensure it aligns with other brand touchpoints (website, etc.)
  • Verify it follows your company's brand guidelines
